The Challenge

Norway is falling behind in the
competition for productions

While neighboring countries strengthen their film incentives, Norway stands still. The result: productions that could create value here are moving abroad.

The Value

Film is good business for all of Norway

For every krone the state refunds through the incentive scheme, 4.7 kroner return to society. At least 1.9 kroner come back directly through taxes and fees.

Our Demands

Four measures that will change everything

01
◆

Predictability

The scheme must have sufficient and predictable funding so productions can plan long-term. Today's uncertainty drives away investments.

02
◆

International Competitiveness

The incentive level must increase to at least 30% to match neighboring countries. 25% is not enough when Iceland offers 40% and Finland 30%.

03
◆

Access for Norwegian Projects

Norwegian productions with international potential must also be able to use the scheme, not just foreign productions.

04
◆

Sustainable Production

The incentive scheme should promote environmentally friendly film production and help the Norwegian industry lead the green transition.
